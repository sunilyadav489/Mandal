About Shree Nakoda Mitra Mandal - Mumbai (NMM)
Shree Nakoda Mitra Mandal – Mumbai (NMM – M) is an Social Organization established in the year 1988 of entire Jain Fraternity residing in Mumbai & hailing from 28 village (i.e. Siwansi, Malani, & Dundara Patti) adjoining Nakodaji Tirth in Rajasthan. NMM – M was founded with the main objective of creating an united, effective & efficient body which bring together & unites  Jain community ( Siwansi ,  Malani & Dundara Patti)  residing in Mumbai to preserve our cultural heritage, promote brotherhood & to serve the community at large.
